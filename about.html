About Master Cove

Master Cove was created to make modern, stylish furniture more accessible.

We saw how expensive furniture has become — and how difficult it is to find clean, good-looking pieces at a fair price. So we built Master Cove around a simple idea: offer well-designed furniture without the traditional retail markup.

By working directly with manufacturing partners, we're able to keep costs down and pass those savings on to you.

What We Offer

Our collection focuses on pieces that work for real life:

  • Modern, clean designs that fit everyday living
  • Functional pieces built for real use
  • Affordable pricing without sacrificing style

We carefully select products that meet our standards for design and practicality — so you're getting pieces that look good and do their job.

Our Model

To keep prices competitive, many of our items are shipped directly from our international partners. This allows us to offer better value compared to traditional furniture stores.

Because of this, shipping times may be longer than local retailers, and inventory may vary based on supplier availability. We believe the tradeoff is worth it: better pricing for the same style and function.

Our Commitment

  • Reliable customer support when you need it
  • Helping you find the right pieces for your space
