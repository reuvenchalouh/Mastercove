Shipping Policy
Last updated: July 5, 2026

At Master Cove, every order ships with free white-glove delivery — no exceptions, no hidden fees. Here's everything you need to know about how your furniture gets to you.

Shipping cost: Free on all orders, every time.

Delivery method: White-glove delivery on all items.

Processing & delivery time: Most items ship within 7–12 business days of order confirmation.

Shipping area: Currently the contiguous United States.

Processing Time

Once your order is placed, we coordinate directly with our furniture suppliers to prepare your item for shipment. Most orders leave the warehouse and are delivered within 7–12 business days. Some made-to-order or high-demand items may take slightly longer — if that's the case for your order, we'll notify you by email.

White-Glove Delivery

All Master Cove furniture ships with white-glove delivery at no additional cost. This means your item will be:

  • Delivered to a room of your choice (not just curbside)
  • Unboxed and inspected upon delivery, when applicable
  • Handled by professional delivery teams trained for furniture

A member of the delivery team will contact you in advance to schedule a delivery window that works for your day.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information where available. You can also check your order status on our Order Tracking page at any time.

Shipping Delays

While we work hard to meet our estimated delivery windows, delays can occasionally occur due to supplier stock availability, weather, or carrier disruptions. If your order is delayed, we will proactively reach out with an updated timeline.

Damaged or Missing Items

If your furniture arrives damaged or a part is missing, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the issue, and we'll work quickly to resolve it — whether that's a replacement part, a full replacement, or a refund.
